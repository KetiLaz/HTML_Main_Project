Okami

Originally a PS2 game, I only had the chance to play it when it came to PS4. Although sometimes its PS2 roots are evident it is such a unique game that you won't care.
The style of the game is inspired by traditional Japanese watercolour art and the world itself is a canvas, manipulated through the player's most valuable tool:
the Celestial Brush. For example using the Celestial Brush you can draw a circle in the sky and turn night into day. Did I mention that you play as a the wolf sun goddess?
Okami is unlike any game I've had played before or have played since.
