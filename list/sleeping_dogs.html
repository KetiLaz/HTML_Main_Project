Sleeping Dogs

This game literally has the same plot as the movie "The Departed". Which is to say the plot is really nothing new and sometimes can feel rushed.
But the combat has an amazing flow with environmental takedowns that are super fun to pull of, and the setting is also very refreshing. Set in contemporary Hong Kong,
it definitely isn't the largest urban game world, but it is a city with countless distractions. Most importanly the city feels alive. Also there are slow motion car chases.
I rest my case.
