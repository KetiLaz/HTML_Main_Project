Journey

You will only need about three hours to complete this game. This is the game I refer to when I say that video games can be art. It is an absolutely stunning game
with a story open to interpretation. There are no rules or instructions or even real objectives in this game. You are free to explore the beautiful world on your own pace.
Journey transcends conventional gaming and becomes more of an interactive experience designed to evoke feelings, thus making it a great gateway fron non players
to get into video games.
