Yakuza 0

This really is a serious mafia drama until the game makes to take a break and sing karaoke that halfway turns into an extravagant music video. At that moment I realised
that this game would be stupid fun. I was not wrong. Fish giant white sharks from a river? Check. Have a disco dance-off? Check. Get a chicken named Nugget to be your manager?
Sure. This game does not take itself seriously in the best possible way and I love it for it.
