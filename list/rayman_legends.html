Rayman Legends

Rayman Legends is one of my comfort games. It is a simple platformer with just three basic mechanics: run, jump, hit.
The excellent level design thought turns the game into a wonderful and fun game. The goal of the game is simple:
save all 700 teensies throught the levels. Of course I spend too many hours finding all 700 of them. Oh, there are also music levels.
Platforming in sync with the music is so satisfying.
