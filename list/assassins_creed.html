Assassin's Creed II

I played this game when I was 13-14 years old and although I grew up playing video games, this was the one that solidified to me that I would be a gamer my whole life.
I finished Assassin's Creed II too many times. I loved the premise, the story but most of all I loved the setting: the height of the Rennaissance in Italy.
Just walking around in Florence and Venice in this game was fun.
