Bloodborne

This game was the first Soulsborne game that I ever played and it will always have a special place in my heart. Like all Soulsborne games it is hard as nails
and I almost quit after I died over and over and over again to the first boss. But after I finally beat him something clicked in me and that was it. I absolutely adore this game
and since I first finished it, I did it all over again and now I'm making a friend of mine play it with me. Again.
